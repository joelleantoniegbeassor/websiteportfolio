David Gees (they/he) is a director*writer*researcher and performer*artist working at the intersection of theater, performance and theory. After working as an intern at Schauspielhaus Zürich during the 2020/21 season, they moved to Vienna to study Cross-Disciplinary Strategies at the University of Applied Arts Vienna. Meanwhile they started to work as a director's assistant at Theater Nestroyhof Hamakom and Schauspielhaus Wien.

In the course of their studies a collaboration with Joëlle Antonie Gbeassor emerged, with whom he produced and co-directed «Daddy's favourite: Monsta Energy: a fairytale» (2023) and «Frankenstein Lost» (2023/24). In 2024, they founded the theatre*performance*production collective dramaj together.

Their previous work includes:
