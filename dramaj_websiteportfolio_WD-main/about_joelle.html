dramaj is a theatre*performance*production collective founded in 2024 and is currently based between Vienna (AT) and Zurich (CH).

This website*portfolio is an impossible attempt of an archival collection of the misty unconditional core we constitute and are constituted around.

«Such mist can be described as the dreamy, foggy, steamy matter, or to put it differently, an uncontrollable and wet foam of imagination».

This uncontrollable wet foam permeates every corner, between and around us, its intangibility is terrifying. Many people and institutions ignore their irrational, foggy core, clinging to stories they can control — tales of fixed places, linear progression, and rigid protocols. This colonial nonsense of a past still haunts our present.

How*Is it possible not to reject but to acknowledge and take care of this foggy, imaginative, dreamy core, that

«is composed of a multitude of fragments, rather than of smooth epistemological wholes. Some fragments will be collected here and bound temporarily together to form a loosely integrated net – or, better, an unassimilated hybrid, a monstrous body».

This monstrous misty core body consists of unanswered questions, late-night conversations, disagreements, unfinished stories, the killed darlings, theory and headaches, warm hugs, WhatsApp-messages, cold after-work-beeeeeers, schedules and imaginary budgets that might at some point trespass the borders of fictionality and manifest in real $$$.

Through sitting with the im*possibility of acknowledging and taking care of the misty unconditional core that immediately slips your grip as you try to grasp it, we learned that

«research does not have to be complete, finished, exhaustive (and exhausting),[our attention was drawn] to the search in research, so this work is at its heart, searching, open ended and generative».

We don’t think it will ever be finished.

And no, we don’t know what we are doing.
