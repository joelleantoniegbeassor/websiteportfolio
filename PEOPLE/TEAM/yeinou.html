eng
Yèinou is a independent performance artist and actor. Yèinou has performed in five productions at the Schauspielhaus Zürich; High on "Hope", "Bullestress", "Ich chan es Zündhölzli ahzünde" (directed by Suna Gürler) as well as "Pinocchio" and "Der Sturm" (directed by Wu Tsang). Yèinou also makes music in the band Manic Pixxies and is currently rehearsing for the next production "Last Night a DJ took my Life" (director: Joana Tischkau).
